Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to create a place where customers can see what the prices are for rafting and be able to contact Down by the Whitewater easily.
This will make it easier for the customers to interact with the company and have all the information they may need in one spot.

Audience

Typography

Heading Font: 'Libre Caslon Text', serif

I felt like this font was a little more on the adventurous side than the normal Times New Roman, but still standard enough to look good.

Paragraph Font: 'Montserrat', sans-serif

I felt like Montserrat was a good accent to the Libre Carlson Text.

Normal paragraph example

The best Whitewater Rafting in Colorado, White Water Rafting Company offers rafting on the Colorado and Roaring Fork Rivers in Glenwood Springs. Since 1974, we have been family owned and operated, rafting the Shoshone section of Glenwood Canyon and beyond.
